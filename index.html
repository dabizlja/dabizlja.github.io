Our Story

A home
in the trees

Nestled within the ancient Lipovica forest on the peaceful outskirts of Belgrade, ForestAura is more than a place to stay — it is an invitation to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with what matters.

Surrounded by tall linden trees and the quiet rhythm of the forest, this thoughtfully crafted retreat offers warmth, privacy, and a stillness that is rare to find so close to the city.

Whether you come to rest, to explore the forest trails, or simply to sit on the terrace with a morning coffee — ForestAura welcomes you home.

What Awaits You

Crafted for
the senses

Every detail at ForestAura has been thoughtfully prepared for your comfort and enjoyment.

Spa Center

Unwind in our spa center — a private sanctuary of warmth and stillness designed to restore both body and mind after a day in the forest.

Pool

Take a refreshing dip or simply float while the sounds of the forest surround you. Our pool is your private retreat under the open sky.

Forest Setting

Wake to birdsong and the scent of linden. The ancient Lipovica forest is yours to wander — trails, clearings, and pure forest air just outside your door.

Kids Playground

A safe, magical space for little ones to play freely and explore their imagination in the fresh forest air, while you unwind nearby.
